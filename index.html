About me

A bit about myself

I'm a Senior Software Engineer with over five years of professional experience designing and shipping full-stack web applications. My journey started with a deep curiosity for how things work on the web, which quickly evolved into a passion for building clean, performant, and maintainable systems.

I thrive at the intersection of product thinking and engineering craft — whether that means architecting a real-time collaborative platform, optimising database queries for sub-50ms response times, or mentoring junior developers through code review. I believe great software is a team sport and that writing code is an act of communication.

Outside of work I contribute to open-source projects, explore new frameworks, speak at local meetups, and occasionally write about software design on my blog. When I'm not behind a keyboard you'll find me hiking the Croatian coast or experimenting with sourdough bread.
